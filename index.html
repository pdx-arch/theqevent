Good questions

Everything you want to know.

How many people can play?+

The Q works beautifully for intimate gatherings and large rooms alike. We’ll tailor the format to your venue, group size, and schedule.

Do guests need to download anything?+

No app required. The Q is a live hosted experience with table-based play and paper answer sheets—simple for guests and seamless for your event.

Can the questions be customized?+

Yes. We can create custom questions that highlight your organization, sponsors, company culture, or event theme.

How do sponsors fit in?+

Sponsors can present individual rounds, the scoreboard, prizes, or the championship round—giving them a meaningful presence all evening.
